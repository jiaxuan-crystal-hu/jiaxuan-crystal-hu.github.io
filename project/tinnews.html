Tin-News

A Tinder-like News App to keep you posted.

Tin-News is an online news application that allow users to swipe and view news. In Tin-News, users can "swipe right" to like or "swipe left" to dislike other a news to personalize their interested news recommendation. Users can also search for topics that they are interested in.

Technical Details

The application Tin-News follows an Instagram flavor in the layout based on Google Component Architectural MVVM Pattern. Components of the application, such as bottom bar and page navigation, utilizes JetPack navigation components. The application employs Mindork's PlaceHolderView to support swipe gestures for liking and/or dislicking a news shown on the screen.

We build the Room Database with LiveData and ViewModel to support local cache and offline model that allows users to view news withou Wi-Fi. The latest news data are pulled from a RESTful endpoint (newsapi.org) with integration of Retrofit and Rxjava.
